Montana is a 2 year old Scotch Collie. She wears several hats around here, but her primary role is that of protector. She protects the veggies from critters, the chickens from predators, our children from anyone new and our house from things that go 'bump' in the night.


Montana will have her first litter at the end of May. She has been bred to Wallace.
