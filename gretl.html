Old Time Scotch Collies

Gretl (call name Lila) from Montana's 2018 litter

Lila lives in Michigan with a teenager who wants to do agility training with her. She loves running the river trail near her house and consistently obeys ‘come’ when off leash. She's more calm in new situations; she used to get pretty excited when younger. Lila is described as alert, sweet and a good companion. She is said to also be VERY aware of her surroundings, intelligent and willing to please. Lila has her challenges with impulse control, she whined to play with other dogs in obedience class and it was too distracting. Lila's owners are having a trainer come privately and it’s made a huge difference. They're considering getting another dog as a companion for Lila because of her desire to play with other dogs. While here at Sunshine Farm, Lila and Maria were the two puppies with the most spunk and interest in people.
