Wallace: sire of Montana's 2018 and 2021 litters

Glen Craigean's Dunrovin Cormac is the sire Montana's 2018 and 2021 litters. Wallace, as he is more affectionately known, is owned by Kayla and Alex Franklin of Marshall, North Carolina.

Wallace is a milk sable, working old time Scotch Collie who is protective of young livestock and who can catch individual chickens without harm and on command. He guards unfenced goats and can put errant goats back in their pasture. He catches farm pests like squirrels and rats. Wallace is loving and protective of his 3 human children charges and is an able swimmer. See Wallace's genetics and photos.
